ArogCura is designed for patients, doctors, healthcare administrators, laboratory professionals, pharmacy staff, and healthcare organizations such as hospitals and clinics.

Yes, ArogCura provides dedicated Android and iOS applications for Patients and Doctors, along with a web portal for all users.

Yes, ArogCura prioritizes data security by adhering to HIPAA standards and employing advanced security measures such as hashing, high-level encryption, and secure decryption methods. This ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and safety of your sensitive healthcare information.

Patients can search for doctors, view their available schedules, and book appointments directly through the platform. Both online teleconsultation and in-person appointments are supported.

Yes, ArogCura allows you to store and access your complete health history, including general health records, medical history, test reports, and prescriptions.

ArogCura supports online consultations through live voice or video calls, offering a convenient way to interact with doctors for health advice. Additionally, patients can use the messaging feature for further clarification or double confirmation on diagnoses and prescriptions. The platform also facilitates seeking a second opinion or consulting with another doctor, ensuring comprehensive care and multiple perspectives for better health outcomes.

Doctors can self-register, manage patient appointments, conduct teleconsultations, access patient health records, and issue ePrescriptions. The platform also helps doctors generate leads through features like “Ask a Doctor.”

Yes, doctors can define their schedules, set availability for appointments, and manage their consultation timings easily.

Yes, ArogCura provides seamless integration with laboratories for test results and pharmacies for prescription fulfillment, enabling a fully digital healthcare workflow.

Yes, the platform includes digital signature functionality for secure ePrescriptions and other healthcare documentation.
