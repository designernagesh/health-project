Patient Relationship Management (CRM)

ArogCura’s Patient Relationship Management (CRM) is a comprehensive and intuitive solution designed to transform the way healthcare providers interact with their patients. By integrating advanced features for health data management, communication, and teleconsultation, the platform creates a seamless and efficient healthcare experience for all stakeholders.

Effortless Patient Self-Registration

ArogCura ensures a quick, secure, and seamless onboarding process for patients, enabling them to access healthcare services with ease:

  • Quick and Secure Setup: Patients can self-register via web or mobile platforms, streamlining the account creation process.
  • Verification for Security:
    • OTP or Email Verification: Ensures data integrity and privacy by confirming the patient’s identity.
    • Captcha Verification:: Prevents automated or fraudulent registrations, enhancing platform security.
  • Aadhaar Integration: Seamless Aadhaar-based verification provides robust identity authentication, ensuring reliability and compliance.
  • Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) Integration: Automated registration with the ABDM system ensures instant and accurate enrollment in India’s national healthcare ecosystem.
  • Health Record Initialization: Patients can start building their health profile immediately, including personal details, health history, and medical records.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Designed for accessibility, allowing patients of all technical levels to register effortlessly.

With these features, ArogCura empowers patients to securely access a wide range of healthcare services, ensuring privacy, reliability, and convenience.

Comprehensive Health History Management

A centralized repository where patients and authorized users can record, manage, and access:

  • General Health Records: Routine health metrics.
  • Specific Health Histories:
    • Medical History: Chronic illnesses, diagnoses, and treatments.
    • Surgical History: Past surgeries with detailed records.
    • Drug History:Prescriptions, including traditional remedies.
    • Allergy Records: Known sensitivities to medications or other factors.
  • Social and Family Health History: Behavioral habits and hereditary conditions.

With these features, ArogCura empowers patients to securely access a wide range of healthcare services, ensuring privacy, reliability, and convenience.

Seamlessly Connecting Patients with Trusted Doctors

ArogCura acts as a reliable bridge, facilitating fast, efficient, and flexible consultations between patients and authorized doctors while extending support for lab tests, radiology services, and pharmacy needs, ensuring complete healthcare access.

  • Appointment (Walk-In):

    Patients can book appointments in available slots to avoid delays and ensure timely care. Health vitals and records are securely stored for future reference, ensuring no crucial information is lost.

  • Scheduled Appointments (Video Call):

    Patients can book video or voice consultations with doctors at pre-scheduled slots, avoiding the need for live availability. This feature ensures structured consultations without the hassle of physical visits or waiting times.

  • Live Teleconsult Call:

    Patients gain instant access to doctors through live video or voice consultations when doctors activate availability, delivering timely medical advice during urgent situations.

  • Second Opinion (Message Exchanges):

    Patients can seek additional opinions from specialists via secure messages with a 24-hour response window. This cost-effective option provides clarity and confidence for better understanding and decision-making regarding health concerns.

  • Pharmacy and Diagnostic Support:

    After completing a doctor’s consultation, patients can seamlessly proceed with lab test bookings, radiology tests, or pharmacy services. Medicines can be delivered directly to their doorstep or made available for convenient pickup, ensuring end-to-end healthcare support.

Data Privacy and Security

At ArogCura, safeguarding your personal health information is our utmost priority. We implement the highest standards of data protection and privacy protocols to ensure your data remains secure and confidential.

  • HIPAA Compliance:

    We adhere strictly to global standards like HIPAA to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of patient data.

  • 256-Bit Encryption:

    All data transfers are protected using advanced 256-bit encryption, ensuring secure communication and safeguarding against breaches.

  • Password Security:

    Even your passwords are stored in a highly secured, encrypted format, ensuring that not even we can access or read them.

  • User-Controlled Data Sharing:

    Patients have full control over who can access their data, empowering them to manage their health information securely.

With ArogCura, your health data is always in safe hands, giving you peace of mind while focusing on your well-being.

Key Features

Third-Party Data Entry Support

ArogCura enables seamless collaboration across healthcare teams, ensuring that patient data is accurate and up-to-date:

  • Streamlined Registration:

    Authorized users such as front-desk staff, clinic/hospital admin users, or nurses can register patients directly into the system, simplifying onboarding processes.

  • Doctor-Centric Updates:

    Doctors can efficiently add patient case notes, diagnosis details, and vitals during or after consultations, ensuring comprehensive records.

  • Administrative Oversight:

    Admin users in clinics or hospitals can input and update necessary patient information, ensuring a complete and unified patient profile.

This multi-user data entry feature reduces redundancies, enhances collaboration, and ensures a holistic view of patient health records.

Graphical Health Insights

ArogCura brings your health data to life with visually engaging and intuitive dashboards:

  • Consolidated Dashboards:

    View your entire health history at a glance through customizable graphs and trends.

  • Psychographic Visualization:

    Gain a deeper understanding of your health with graphical representations of vitals and key metrics, making complex data easy to interpret.

  • Doctor’s Perspective:

    Doctors can access psychographic views of patient data, providing actionable insights for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

Empower yourself and your healthcare providers with clear, data-driven insights for better decision-making and improved outcomes.
